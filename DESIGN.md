# MurciaServers Design System

## Theme

Dark cinematic gaming interface with compact tactical details and image-led map selection.

## Color

- Brand accent: `#ff5500`
- Accent highlight: `#ff955f`
- Accent wash: `rgba(255, 85, 0, 0.12)`
- Background: `#090d10`
- Primary text: `#f3f7f8`
- Muted text: `#929fa6`

Blue or cyan accents must not be introduced. Use the orange brand ramp for interactive, highlighted and focused states.

## Responsive behavior

Every change must support mobile, tablet, laptop and wide desktop layouts. Preserve the existing breakpoints and reduced-motion handling.

## Components

Keep the established sharp seven-pixel radius, dark elevated surfaces, restrained borders, image-backed map cards and orange hover/focus feedback.
