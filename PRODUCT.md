# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Call of Duty Zombies players browsing maps, records and competitive statistics across mobile and desktop devices.

## Product Purpose

Provide a fast, atmospheric map browser that makes Zombies records easy to discover and share.

## Brand Personality

Competitive, tactical and energetic.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS styling, bright white dashboards, playful rounded interfaces and decorative color unrelated to navigation or state.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the dark cinematic Zombies atmosphere.
- Keep maps and records immediately scannable.
- Use the brand accent sparingly for navigation, interaction and emphasis.
- Maintain full responsive behavior with every change.
- Prefer clarity and speed over decorative complexity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast, visible interaction states, keyboard navigation and reduced-motion support.
